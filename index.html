How to become a real Scotsman

  • Say lassie or girrrrl
  • Say wee instead of little
  • Wear a kilt, a flat hat and long white socks
  • Live in Scotland
  • Eat yer porridge
  • Try and live in a cottage
  • Sing Scottish songs
  • Say bonny instead beautiful
  • Make sure you have a Scottish accent
  • Try Haggis at least once
  • Play the bagpipes
  • Learn to Ceilidh dance
